Pillar of "Bangladesh 2.0"

Innovation

Innovation is defined as the process of bringing about new ideas, methods, products, services, or solutions that have a significant positive impact and value.

Sustainability

Sustainability is a social goal that aims to ensure people can live on Earth for a long time. In addition to natural resources, we also need social and economic resources.

Infrastructure

Infrastructure is the basic physical systems of a business, region, or nation and often involves the production of public goods or production processes.

Empowerment

Empowerment means people having power and control over their own lives. Enables them to represent their interests in a self-determined way.
